Job Summary

A post graduate in Physics with Special Electronics from Madras Christian College, Chennai, and holds a Masters in Education. A true leader who has achieved many a milestone in her career spanning over a period of 30 years from being a teacher to becoming the Principal of RCIS-Kalyan Nagar. Before stepping into the portals of RCIS-K, Mrs. Rekha Vinod was the Principal of Air Force School, Srinagar for 2 years. She joined RCIS-K as an Academic Co-ordinator in the year 2006. She went on to become the Vice Principal in 2011 and Principal in 2015. The growth of the school is the testimony of her perseverance and success in the field of education. As a Principal, she has been finetuning and implementing transformative educational curriculum incubating an education, more suited to the needs of children in the 21st century. The edifice of her efforts are constructed on universal values and service to the society. She has worked in the capacity of the Secretary, BSSCA (Bangalore Sahodaya Schools Complex Association) from June 2018 to May 2021 holding various academic enrichment assignments for collaborating with CBSE schools at the State and the National level. She has indeed exemplified the proverb ‘Action speaks louder than words’. Her long association with RCIS-K since the inception, her consistent guidance, leadership and far sight has enabled us face challenges, restore values and build students who have risen to power and distinction.
